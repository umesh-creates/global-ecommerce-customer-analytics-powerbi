# 📊 Global E-Commerce Customer Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze customer behavior, purchasing patterns, loyalty, fraud detection, and churn risk for a global e-commerce business.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides business insights into customer performance across different countries, categories, and demographics. It helps identify customer trends, fraud distribution, loyalty levels, and churn risk through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📁 Dataset Features

The dataset contains customer-level information including:

- Customer ID
- Age
- Gender
- Country
- Average Order Value
- Total Orders
- Last Purchase
- Fraud Status
- Preferred Category
- Email Open Rate
- Customer Since
- Loyalty Score
- Churn Risk

---

## 📈 Dashboard KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Loyalty Score
- Average Churn Risk
- Average Email Open Rate

---

## 📊 Visualizations

- Revenue by Country
- Customers by Join Year
- Churn Rate by Age Group
- Loyalty Score & Email Open Rate by Country
- Preferred Category Ranking
- Fraud vs Genuine Customers
- Customer Distribution by Gender
- Churn Risk by Gender
- Email Open Rate by Gender
